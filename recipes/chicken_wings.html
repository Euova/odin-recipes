Buffalo Chicken Wings

Description:

In this meal, butter and vinegar-and-cayenne-pepper hot sauce are combined together to cover deep-fried, unbreaded chicken wings or drumsticks.

Common accompaniments include celery and a blue cheese dipping sauce, which cools the palate.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg chicken wings, wingtips removed
  • 1 tablesppon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder

Buffalo Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of unsalted melted butter
  • 1/4 cup of hot sauce
  • 1 tablesppon of sugar

Steps:

  1. Dry the chicken completely with a paper towel. Set the oven to 230Â° C. A wire rack should be positioned on top of a baking sheet with a rim and lined with foil.
  2. In a bowl, combine the salt, garlic powder, and baking powder. Sprinkle the mixture over the chicken and toss to blend. Place the chicken on the wire rack you've prepared.
  3. The chicken should be baked for 25 minutes, turned over, and baked for a further 25 minutes, or until crisp and well done.
  4. Sauce ingredients should be combined in a medium basin. Transfer the chicken to a bowl from the baking sheet. Over the chicken, drizzle the sauce. Toss the chicken in the sauce to coat it. Serve with the dipping sauce of your choice.
