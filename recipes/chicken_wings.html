Buffalo Chicken Wings

Description:

In this meal, butter and vinegar-and-cayenne-pepper hot sauce are combined together to cover deep-fried, unbreaded chicken wings or drumsticks.

Common accompaniments include celery and a blue cheese dipping sauce, which cools the palate.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg chicken wings, wingtips removed
  • 1 tablesppon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder

Buffalo Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of unsalted melted butter
  • 1/4 cup of hot sauce
  • 1 tablesppon of sugar
