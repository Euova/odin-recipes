Quick Delicious Ramen

Description:

This Japanese noodle dish combines a flavorful broth, a choice of noodles, and a range of meats or vegetables.

Since the broth is clear and the noodles are often thin curly noodles, the flavor is typically salty and light.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 3 packages of instant ramen
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Steps:

  1. In a sizable saucepan, add the eggs and 1 inch of cold water. 1 minute of boiling and cooking is required. Remove eggs from the heat and cover with a tight-fitting lid. Leave eggs alone for 8 to 10 minutes. Before peeling and halving, thoroughly drain and allow to cool.
  2. Over medium heat, warm the olive oil in a sizable Dutch oven or stockpot. Add the ginger and garlic, and simmer for 1-2 minutes, stirring often, until fragrant.
  3. Stir in 3 cups of water, soy sauce, and chicken broth.
  4. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 10 minutes or so. For two to three minutes, stir in the ramen packages until they are loose and well cooked.
  5. The green onions should be stirred for around 2 minutes.
  6. With eggs as a garnish, serve right away.
