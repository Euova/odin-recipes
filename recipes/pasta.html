Pasta with Fresh Tomato Sauce

Description:

An emblematic Italian meal made of spaghetti, tomatoes, and basil, which are native to the country. The traditional dish of spaghetti and tomato sauce is straightforward but everything but insignificant.

To achieve the ideal taste harmony, level of creaminess, and sweetness, there are countless adjustments that may be made, beginning with the tomato variety.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shallot
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 225 g of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 handful of fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons of capers
  • 30 g of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 110 g spaghetti
