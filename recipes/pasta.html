Pasta with Fresh Tomato Sauce

Description:

An emblematic Italian meal made of spaghetti, tomatoes, and basil, which are native to the country. The traditional dish of spaghetti and tomato sauce is straightforward but everything but insignificant.

To achieve the ideal taste harmony, level of creaminess, and sweetness, there are countless adjustments that may be made, beginning with the tomato variety.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shallot
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 225 g of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 handful of fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons of capers
  • 30 g of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 110 g spaghetti

Steps:

  1. A pot should be filled with water, sprinkled with salt, and placed over high heat.
  2. Finely dice 1 shallot.
  3. Four garlic cloves should be thinly sliced.
  4. 500 ml of cherry tomatoes should be cut in half and placed in a bowl.
  5. Cut the basil leaves into half a handful.
  6. With a generous splash of olive oil, heat a sizable skillet over medium heat.
  7. Add a pinch of salt and your shallot, garlic, and other ingredients to the heating pan. Spend approximately 3 to 4 minutes sweating till soft.
  8. Add your cherry tomatoes when they have softened, then season with salt. Add 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes. Place a lid on top.
  9. 110g of spaghetti should be added.
  10. Just before the pasta is finished cooking, conserve about 125 milliliters of the cooking water before draining the spaghetti.
  11. To make a thick goop, take off the cover and mash the tomatoes with a wooden spoon. Add pasta water until it reaches the desired consistency.
  12. Add 1/2 a handful of entire basil leaves and 1 tablespoon of capers per person. Stir continuously until the required consistency is reached.
  13. Then, add your spaghetti and combine everything with a fork. Kill the heat.
  14. Place some spaghetti on a plate, then top with parmesan.
